MOSCOW

Moscow is the capital and largest city of Russia. The city stands on the Moskva River in Central Russia,
with a population estimated at 13.0 million residents within the city limits, over 18.8 million residents in the urban
area, and over 21.5 million residents in the metropolitan area. The city covers an area of 2,511 square kilometers,
while the urban area covers 5,891 square kilometers, and the metropolitan area covers over 26,000 square kilometers.

WHEN TO GO TO MOSCOW

The best time to visit Moscow is April and May, when the temperature creeps into the 50s and 60s,
the sun begins to shine for significant portions of the day, and hotel rates have yet to skyrocket into peak
ranges. Of course, the golden period is summer, when the city is warm and bustling.
